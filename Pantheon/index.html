How to Play

Game Objective

Be the first player to complete three cards that all share the same element.

Elements

  • Earth
  • Air
  • Fire
  • Water

Turn Actions

  • Attempt Challenge: Try to complete an Active Card's challenge
  • Forsake: Discard an Active Card and draw a new one
  • Trade: Exchange cards with another player
  • Skip: Pass your turn

About Pantheon

A competitive card-based challenge game with celestial themes

Pantheon is a strategic card game where players complete challenges while managing disadvantages.

Game Assets

  • 24 Zodiac Cards
  • 2 Retrograde Cards
