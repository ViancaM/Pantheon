How to Play Pantheon

Goal

Be the first player to complete three cards that all share the same element (Earth, Air, Fire, or Water).

Elements

  • Earth
  • Air
  • Fire
  • Water

Setup

  1. All cards are shuffled together.
  2. Each player draws 3 cards as their Active Cards.
  3. The remaining cards form the Deck.
  4. Each player may use one Retrograde in the game.

Card Types

Active Cards

  • These are 3 cards in the player's hand
  • Each has a challenge to complete
  • Until completed, the disadvantage is in effect

Completed Cards

  • Cards that have had their challenge successfully completed
  • Kept face-up in front of the player

Retrograde Cards

  • When used, all players' Completed Cards return to the deck
  • Each player may only use this effect once

Turn Actions

Each turn, a player can do one of these:

  • Choose 1 Active Card and attempt its challenge
    • If completed → move to Completed Cards
    • If failed → apply its disadvantage
  • Forsake (Discard) an Active card and draw a single card from the deck
  • Trade with the opposing player
  • Skip turn (cannot skip more than once in a row)

Winning

A player wins when they have three Completed Cards of the same element.
